Hello!

I am a PhD student working with the Flight Dynamics & Control/Hybrid Systems Lab. I am interested in statistical methods in estimation & control.

Prior to this, I worked with the MathWorks Inc. for a little less than a year at Natick, Massachusetts. I did my MS in Aeronautics & Astronautics Engineering from Purdue University, and BTech in Aerospace Engineering from IIT Kanpur.
Interests. Statistical methods in estimation & control
